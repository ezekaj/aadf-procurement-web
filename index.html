The Challenge

Problem & Solution

// Problem

Procurement processes are manual, opaque, and time-consuming. Evaluating proposals across dozens of vendors with inconsistent criteria leads to suboptimal selections, compliance risks, and wasted resources.

// Solution

An AI-driven procurement platform that automates tender management, analyzes proposals intelligently, matches vendors to requirements algorithmically, and provides transparent evaluation scoring -- all from a unified dashboard.

Junction Tirana Hackathon
